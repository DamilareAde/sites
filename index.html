About The Placement

My industrial training was conducted at HiiT Plc (Lekki Phase 2 Centre), Nigeria's leading ICT training institute. The program was designed to provide intensive hands-on experience in software development and cybersecurity.

Under the mentorship of Mrs. Timilehin Whetode and other senior instructors, I completed the Professional Diploma in Software Development, mastering technologies ranging from UI/UX Design to Mobile App Development with Flutter.
